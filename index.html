Design

Design is a broad and multidimensional concept that encompasses various disciplines and fields. At its core, design involves the process of creating and planning to achieve a specific purpose or solve a particular problem. It is a methodical and intentional approach to problem-solving, often with the goal of producing a functional and aesthetically pleasing outcome.

Development

Web development refers to the process of building and maintaining websites or web applications. It involves a variety of tasks, including designing, coding, testing, and debugging, with the goal of creating a functional and visually appealing online presence. Web development can range from simple static web pages to complex dynamic web applications.
