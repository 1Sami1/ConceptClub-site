How to Create a Brand

Tiffany Chan

February 22, 2024

Want to create a brand but don't know where to start? No worries, were here to help!

Creating a brand is not as difficult as you think, to solidify a strong brand presence compile a brand book. Simply follow these steps in creating one, and your brand will be ready in no timeâ€¦

  1. Brainstorming: Brand Basics - Before we begin to create a brand we must think about what our brand will be about. Without a clear idea of your brand, it will be difficult to determine target audiences and others.

    2. A few important things to brainstorm about are areas of business or the industry that your brand will be positioned in, a product or service that you will sell or offer, and of course your brand name. Once you have those ideas down, you need to think about brand personality/identity, values, positioning, target market, and voice

  2. Brainstorming: Brand Story - The next step is to create a story for your brand. Keep these main ideas in mind to include in the brand story. Using the information above as a reference will also help:
  • Purpose: What is the brand's mission? Your vision for the future? Core values? What is the brand's secondary goal other than product/service promotion?

This is essential because your target audience will want to know why or in what way your product or service can be beneficial to them.

  • Unique Selling Proposition (USP): How is your brand different from the other, such as your competitors?

Letting people know how you stand out from competitors will attract ideal customers:

  • History: How was this brand created? What inspired you to start a brand? Any important part of the progress you want to highlight?

Individuals enjoy learning about brand histories; your audience gains interesting information about the brand just from reading your story.

  • Brand Identity: What are some words that describe your brand?

Choosing the right words can make it easier for people to associate and remember your brand long after they visit your website or social media. Make sure to choose words that will suit you, keeping your brand values in mind can be helpful too.

  • Mood Board: Search for images, textures, patterns, and fonts that you think suit the brand, put them together in a collage. It is also recommended to choose colors that resonate with your brand.

Creating a mood board will help you visualize your brand better, relating to your overall brand image and identity.

  • Logo creation: Create quick sketches of your logo ideas, then select your top 2-3 choices. Refine your favorites further. Once you have your logo sketched, you can make it come to life in photoshop or illustrator.

Your logo will be how your brand will be remembered and recognized. When deciding on a logo, think about if it suits your target audience.

Compile these creations into a document in InDesign or Canva, this is the beginning of your brand book.
Continue refining your imagery, logos, mockups and brand identity. From this you can launch your socials, website and products with a cohesive brand image - leaving a lasting impression on your target audience.
